歡迎來到TASA! / Welcome to TASA! Taiwanese American Student Association aka TASA is a student organization at The Ohio State University for those interested in examining the many facets of the Taiwanese American identity. TASA is open to anyone regardless of identity. We prohibit discrimination on the basis of age, ancestry, color, disability, gender identity or expression, genetic information, HIV/AIDS status, military status, national origin, race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, protected veteran status
